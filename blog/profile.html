Profile

Main researches

David Martin focuses on the interplay between gut microbiota, metabolism, and physical activity. His research combines bioinformatics tools with experimental approaches such as fecal microbiota transplantation (FMT) in mice.

His work highlights how intense exercise can alter microbial diversity and function, influencing insulin sensitivity and muscle glycogen storage. Using data science and omics methods, he explores how microbiome composition impacts health and performance.

Martin's GitHub repositories reflect his commitment to open science and reproducibility in microbiome research, with ongoing projects bridging computational biology and experimental physiology.
